Paired Engineering is meant to support practical, serious, and useful discussion about AI enablement in software delivery.

Expected behavior

  • be respectful, curious, and evidence-aware
  • critique ideas, designs, and claims without attacking people
  • welcome disagreement, but keep it grounded and constructive
  • respect privacy, confidentiality, and context boundaries

Unacceptable behavior

  • harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
  • hateful or discriminatory language
  • doxxing or sharing identifying information without permission
  • repeated bad-faith argument, trolling, or deliberate misrepresentation
  • using the project to promote unsafe, deceptive, or exploitative rollout behavior

Moderation

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

Reporting

If a conduct issue is sensitive, do not post private details publicly. Use the support path described in SUPPORT.md and request a private channel if needed.
